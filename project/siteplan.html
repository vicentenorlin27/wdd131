Website Planning Document: Vicente Construction LLC

Prepared by: Norlin Vicente Montenegro

Site Name

Vicente Construction LLC - General Contractor & Subcontracting Services

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to market Vicente Construction LLC as both a professional General Contractor managing complete residential and commercial builds, and a premier specialized Subcontracting partner. The site showcases our comprehensive expertise across commercial/residential door packages, metal and wood framing, drywall installation/finishing, trim, baseboards, and finish carpentry work.

Target Audience

Primary Audience: Commercial General Contractors, commercial project administrators, multi-family property managers, developers, and residential property owners looking for high-quality, professional interior contracting services.

Three Persona Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: A commercial General Contractor needs to hire a reliable subcontractor to take over a division 8 door, hardware, and interior framing package for an office remodel.
  2. Scenario 2: A residential property developer needs a skilled contractor to manage an interior build-out, including structural framing, drywall hanging, finishing, and trim installations.
  3. Scenario 3: A facility manager needs an on-call contracting team to handle regular handyman repairs, baseboard replacements, door adjustments, and drywall patching.

Color Palette

Primary Accent: Industrial Amber (#d97706) - Construction safety and main actions.

Secondary Base: Steel Slate Navy (#0f172a) - Professional trust and structural authority.

Background Canvas: Soft Off-White (#f8fafc) - Clean, accessible readability.

Typography (Fonts)

Heading Font: 'Segoe UI', Tahoma, sans-serif - Crisp and architectural.

Body Font: Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif - Legible and clean for structured service lists.
